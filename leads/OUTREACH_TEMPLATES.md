## 🎬 Discovery Call Framework (Once You Get Meeting)

### Agenda (15 minutes)
1. **Warm-up (2 min):** Ask about their business, growth goals
2. **Problem dig (5 min):** "Walk me through a typical week. Where do you lose the most time?"
3. **Show relevance (3 min):** Share one example of [their segment] using automation
4. **Qualification (3 min):** Assess if CEO Autónomo is fit
5. **Next step (2 min):** Calendar a deeper demo if qualified

### Key Questions to Ask
- "How many [clients/team members/stores] are you managing right now?"
- "What's your biggest bottleneck when things scale?"
- "How much time/week does [specific admin task] take?"
- "What tools are you using for [their key workflow]?"
- "If you could automate one thing, what would free up the most time?"

### Red Flags (Not a Good Fit)
- No team/no scale yet (too early)
- Already invested in competing automation (sticky)
- No willingness to discuss pain (not motivated)
- Business model doesn't match (out of scope)

### Green Flags (Good Fit)
- Explicitly mentions "we're doing X manually"
- Already using 3+ tools to manage workflows
- Frustrated with Excel/spreadsheet management
- Recent business growth (new pain points)
- Open to exploring solutions

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ **Personalize the first 2 lines** - Shows you researched them  
✅ **Lead with time savings** - Most compelling metric for busy founders/operators  
✅ **Ask permission before selling** - "Worth 15 mins to explore?" → Higher response rates  
✅ **Mention specific segment examples** - "I just helped another [agency/SaaS] do X"  
✅ **Make asks small & easy** - 15 mins > 30 min pitch  
✅ **Follow-up is where conversions happen** - Don't give up at first "no"  
✅ **A/B test subject lines** - Small changes = big impact (30-40% variance)
