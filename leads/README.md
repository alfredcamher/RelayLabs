## 💡 Success Tips

✅ **Personalize everything** - Generic messages get 0% response  
✅ **Lead with pain, not features** - "You're losing 10 hrs/week" beats "Our tool is great"  
✅ **Ask permission** - "Worth 15 mins?" converts better than direct pitch  
✅ **Follow up 3x** - 60% of conversions come from follow-up #2 or #3  
✅ **Track everything** - You can't optimize what you don't measure  
✅ **Personalize at scale** - Use templates but customize first 2 lines

## 📈 Metrics to Track

- **Response rate** - % of outreach that gets replies
- **Meeting rate** - % of responses that book meetings
- **Qualification rate** - % of meetings that are qualified prospects
- **Conversion rate** - % of qualified prospects → customers
- **Cost per lead** - If using paid ads later
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** - Total cost to acquire 1 customer

**Benchmark:** 10% response rate → 30% meeting rate → 60% qualification rate → 20% conversion = 0.36% of outreach becomes customers (18 leads → 1 customer from 50-lead list)
