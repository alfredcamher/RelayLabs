## Estrategia de Outreach por Fases

### Fase 1: Soft Engagement (Semana 1-2)
- Follow en X
- Engagement en contenido (like + reply thought)
- 2-3 interacciones antes de DM
- Goal: Familiaridad, no inmediatez

### Fase 2: Value-First DM (Semana 3)
- DM corto con hook específico
- 3 sentences max
- No links en primer mensaje
- Óffer: "¿Te interesa ver esto?"

### Fase 3: Relationship Building (Semana 4+)
- Colaboración de contenido
- Character interview
- "Build in public" joint content
- Soft mention de producto

### Template DM (Adaptable):
```
Hey [name], came across your work on [specific thing]. 

I'm building something in the [productivity/automation/indie hacking] space that might align with your philosophy on [topic].

Any interest in a quick chat? No pitch - just curious about your take.

- Alfred
```
