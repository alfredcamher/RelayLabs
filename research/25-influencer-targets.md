## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Engagement (Semana 1-2)
- Like + thought-out reply a 5-10 tweets por día
- Build recognition antes del DM
- Engage en threads de valor

### Phase 2: Soft Outreach (Semana 3-4)
- DM a 5 targets por semana
- Template: "Hey [Name], love your work on [specific thing]. Just built something you might find interesting - [one sentence value prop]. No pitch, just thought you'd appreciate. [Link]"

### Phase 3: Collaboration (Mes 2+)
- Guest thread exchanges
- Newsletter mentions
- Affiliate program (50% commission)
