# Medinexo — Rodrigo's Brief (2026-02-05)

## Company Definition
**Medinexo** = Global collaboration + health tech network connecting healthcare silos (doctors, clinics, hospitals, pharma sponsors) for clinical research.

**Thesis:** Eliminate geographic, technological, and bureaucratic barriers in clinical research via technology bridge between supply (research sites/centers) and demand (pharmaceutical sponsors).

**NOT a CRO** — doesn't take budget cuts or intervene in trial finances.

---

## Network Status
- **35+ countries** global presence
- **1,800+ healthcare centers** (growing daily)
- **Thousands of sponsors** (pharma, labs, CROs, medical device companies)
- **"Prime" tier:** Validated sites meeting international standards

## Revenue Model (3 streams)

### 1. RECURRING (Subscriptions)

#### **FOR SITES (Healthcare Providers)**
- **Cost:** $99/month (annual contract) or $125/month (flexible)
- **Services:**
  - **ResearchNexo PRIME:** Certifies center for international sponsors
  - **Direct sponsor connection:** Bypasses CROs, eliminates middleman fees
  - **RWD Data Monetization:** Monetize patient data legally via Federated Computing
    - Data pricing: $5–$2,000+/patient/month (depends on type, quality, rarity)
    - Medinexo = platform/payment manager (like eBay/PayPal)
  - **Data Readiness Assessment:** FREE preliminary statistical analysis of site's data value
  - **Training (e-Learning):** FDA/EMA compliance certifications
  - **Tech Partner Integration:** Access to partner tools (EHR, EMR, CTMS, telemedicine, etc.)

#### **FOR SPONSORS (Pharma, Labs, CROs, Medical Device)**
- **Cost:** $69.99/month (1 person) or $485/month (team of 10)
- **Services:**
  - **Sponsor PRIME Site Search:** Advanced search based on real data (recruitment capacity, infrastructure, experience)
  - **RWD/RWE Access:** Marketplace to request ultra-segmented patient data for feasibility/market analysis
  - **Digital Onboarding:** Reduces site activation from months → days
  - **Compliance Guarantee:** BlueCloud verification (credentials, regulatory compliance)
  - **Cost Reduction:** Direct connections eliminate inflated CRO fees

#### **FOR TECHNOLOGY PARTNERS**
- **Cost:** ~$12,000/month to promote + integrate solutions
- **Commission:** ~10% on each member sale
- **Solutions:** EHR, EMR, CTMS, telemedicine, recruitment, patient management, etc.

### 2. TRANSACTIONAL

- **RWD Data:** Management fee % on every data access transaction (between Site and Sponsor)
- **Technology:** ~10% commission on partner solution sales to members

### 3. PROJECT (One-Time)

- **Data Readiness Services (PAID):** Detailed data provenance, quality, harmonization, normalization
  - **Tier A (High Readiness):** $6,000–$10,000
  - **Tier B (Moderate Readiness):** $11,000–$20,000
  - **Tier C (Low Readiness):** $20,000–$35,000
  - Typical avg ticket: **$50,000 USD per project**

## Key Innovation: RWD/RWE Federated Computing

**What it does:**
- Sites can monetize patient clinical data 100% legally + securely (harmonized, anonymized)
- Sponsors get ultra-segmented, guaranteed-quality data for study feasibility before committing to full trial
- Dramatically accelerates decision-making for sponsors
- Opens new revenue stream for healthcare providers

**Value:** Ultra-fast, ultra-cheap feasibility studies vs. traditional clinical trials
