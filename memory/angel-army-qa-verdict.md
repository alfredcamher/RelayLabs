## LEARNING SYNTHESIS (Executive)

### What We Learned

**1. New Funding Pattern: Credibility > Capital**
- Angel Army inverts traditional fundraising: get network first, capital follows
- A $1k check from credible operator (Coinbase) > $100k from non-network person
- For AI infrastructure: credibility co-signs are worth 10x more than capital

**2. Speed Optimization via Distributed Capital**
- 20-30 micro angels ($1k-$5k) in parallel beats 2-3 mega angels sequentially
- Each angel generates 3-5 warm intros
- Timeline: 10 weeks vs. 6 months traditional
- Network compounds exponentially

**3. Tier System Structure**
- **Tier 1 (Credibility):** Big names, small checks ($5k) → 4-5 people
- **Tier 2 (Network):** Amplifiers ($2.5k) → 8-10 people
- **Tier 3 (Operators):** Technical value ($1k) → 10-15 people
- Targeting prevents spray-and-pray paralysis

**4. "Butter Up" = Structured Relationship Building**
- Frame as joining the team before mega valuation, not "give us money"
- Emphasize their specific value (network, credibility, expertise)
- Positions angels as co-conspirators, not checklist items
