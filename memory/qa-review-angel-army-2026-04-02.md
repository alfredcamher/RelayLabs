## LEARNING SYNTHESIS

### What New Patterns Were Learned? 🎯

**Pattern 1: Credibility > Capital (in pre-seed)**
- Traditional wisdom: "Get the biggest check first"
- Angel Army wisdom: "Get the best network first"
- **Application:** A $1k check from Coinbase PM > $100k check from dentist (no network)
- **Why this matters:** In AI infra, co-signs from recognized names (Stripe, Anthropic, Figma) are worth 10x more than capital
- **For Relay Labs:** Target ex-OpenAI/Anthropic engineers even for small checks

**Pattern 2: Speed optimization via distributed capital**
- Instead of 1-2 mega angels (slow due diligence), get 20-30 micro angels (parallel)
- Each angel nets you 3-5 new warm intros
- Network compounds: Angel 1 (5 intros) → Angels 2-5 (20 intros) → VCs now coming to you
- **Timeline:** 10 weeks to army mobilization vs. 6 months traditional
- **For Relay Labs:** Parallel angel sourcing while building product/raising awareness

**Pattern 3: Deliberate Tier System (not spray-and-pray)**
- Tier 1 (Credibility): Big names, small checks ($5k)
- Tier 2 (Network): Influencers, amplifiers ($2.5k)
- Tier 3 (Operators): Technical help, product feedback ($1k)
- **Why tiers work:** Limits decision paralysis, enables targeted outreach, builds momentum visibly
- **For Relay Labs:** Identify 5-8 Tier 1 targets immediately (ex-AI team founders)

**Pattern 4: "Butter Up" as structured relationship building**
- Not "ask for money"
- Instead: "Join the team before the mega valuation"
- Emphasis: your specific credibility, what they add (not what you gain)
- **Frame shift:** They become co-conspirators, not investors
- **For Relay Labs:** Position angels as "founding operators" not "pre-seed backers"
