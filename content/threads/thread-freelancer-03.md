## Tweet 4/9 - What Changed

We worked with a copywriter who:
- Was doing $6k/month (freelance work)
- Spending 20 hours/week on admin
- Actual billable capacity: 20 hours/week (capped at $8k potential)

We automated her admin with an autonomous agent:

1. **Client intake:** Agent reads inquiries, qualifies leads, proposes timeline + rate
2. **Project tracking:** Auto-updates Notion board with deadlines
3. **Invoicing:** Agent generates invoice reminders at day 25+ (gets paid 3x faster)
4. **Content calendar:** AI drafts her weekly newsletter (she just approves)

Result month 1:
- Admin time: 20 hours → 3 hours/week
- Billable capacity: Back to 35 hours/week
- Revenue potential: $6k → $10.5k/month
- Actual revenue month 2: $10.2k (client wins from faster response)
