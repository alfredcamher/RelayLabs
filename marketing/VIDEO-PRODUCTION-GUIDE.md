## Recording Setup

### Camera Position
- Eye level
- 3-4 feet away
- Rule of thirds (slightly off-center)
- Plain background or branded background

### Audio
- Clap before each take for sync
- Lavalier at chest level
- Check levels: -12dB to -6dB peak
- No echo (add blankets if needed)

### Lighting
- Window = key light (face camera from window)
- No harsh shadows
- Ring light for fill ($30 Amazon)

---

## Editing Workflow

### DaVinci Resolve (Free)

**Import:**
- Drag media to Media Pool
- Drag clips to timeline

**Cut:**
- Blade tool (B)
- Cut dead space, "ums", mistakes
- Target: Remove 20-30% of raw footage

**Color:**
- Color tab → Auto Color
- Adjust exposure if needed
- Saturation: +10% Boost
- Output: 1080p, 24fps, H.264

**Export Settings:**
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Frame rate: 24
- Quality: YouTube 1080p preset

## Thumbnail Design (Canva)

### Formula Ganadora
1. **Tu cara** (expresión emocional - sorpresa/concerned)
2. **Texto grande** (3-5 palabras maximo)
3. **Elemento visual** (flecha, grafico, icono)
4. **Color contrastante** (fondo azul, texto naranja/blanco)

### Plantilla Canva Gratis
- Buscar: "YouTube Thumbnail"
- Size: 1280x720
- Fuente: Bebas Neue o Montserrat Bold
- Colores: #2563eb (azul) + #f59e0b (naranja)

### Textos de Thumbnail
- "El Error #1"
- "Delega o Muere"
- "$15K/año"
- "70h → 25h"
- "2 Min Rule"
